Gaming For Kids

A charity for less fortunate kids that helps give them access to video games, so they can enjoy them like everyone else.

What is our goal?

Our goal is to gather donations and resources to be able to spread the love of video games and fun to all of the children who do not have access to them.

How can you help?

There are several ways you can help. You can donate any amount of money, you can donate gaming consoles, games, and anything else you would think these children would enjoy.
